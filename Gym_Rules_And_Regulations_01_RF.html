8K24 Fitness Elite

Membership Rules And Regulations

Members must have an Access Card Issued by 8K24 Fitness Elite Gym in order to access the premises.

1. A member may use the Gym during any operating hours.

2. Members are not allowed to bring guests.

3. Members must abide by all membership and facility rules and regulations.

4. A member who allows another person to use his/her card will have his/her membership privilege revoked.

5. Membership is not refundable or transferable. A lost card may be replaced by presentation of this contract and of the card payment for P500.

6. Members must be at least 18 years old. Members 17 and below must be accompanied by parents / guardian at all times.

7. Report any damaged or malfunctioning equipment to a staff member immediately.

8. Do not drop weights and equipment to their proper racks after used.

9. Return all weights equipment to their proper after use.

10. Proper workout attire and footwear are required.

11. Do not bring food into the fitness area.

12. Alcohol, Tobacco products and illegal drugs (including anabolic steroids) are prohibited.

13. No Pets shall be permitted on the Property. All members shall obey this for cleanliness and the convenience of other Member.

For the interest of member comfort and safety, children are NOT allowed on the premises.

Before signing these documents, I have read, understood and hereby agreed to the terms and conditions of the membership.
